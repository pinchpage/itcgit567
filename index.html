A small and personal note from the team.

"Ah! another crowdfunding note, eh?" I know it is running now in many of your minds. But, yes, this is yet another crowdfunding note. And, we have a reason. And, the reason: we failed. Yes, you've read it right. We failed.

We had the best tool to win: the idea of this film, yet we failed. We wanted to tell this story in its original and raw form, and we saw failure coming towards us whenever we tried it. We sat ourselves down, and looked at eachother's faces; we knew it. We didn't want to be failures anymore. We know you are there for us. You are there for us to feel our narration, and we know that you like the emotion in the rawest form. Prove us correct, and together we would win this. And, if you want to be with us in this journey, you could fund us. We have various options on how to fund. Get in touch with us and we'll see how we could take this relation further.

Sushruth J

Director

Sushruth is a writer turned director. He has written for films in Telugu film Industry. Besides, he has also written screeplay for a few webseries, and has a shortfilm to his directional credit. "Doped", a short film directed by Sushruth has won many awards including "Best picture" and "Best director" in various film festivals. This aside, he is from Hyderabad. After his Post-graduation in Pharamaceutical Chemistry from BITS Pilani, he has worked for Novartis Healthcare in Hyderabad. Currently, he works for Glaxo Smith Kline in Bangalore. Jerry Vincent

Original Music and Background Score

Jerry Vincent is a music director, and if you have heard any of AR Rahman's songs since "Rockstar", you are quite probably listening to Jerry's work in some or the other way. He works as a music producer, music programmer and sound engineer at Panchathan Record Inn, Chennai. He is one of the members of the ensemble Qutub-e-Kripa, who has done background scores of the films like 24, Ok Bangaram (Tamil: Oh Kaadal Kanmani, Hindi: Ok Jaanu), Tamasha, Mom, Cheliyaa (Tamil: Kaatru Veliyidai). His recent composition "Have you ever wondered" from the film Lake of Fire has been shortlisted for the 90th Academy Awards in the Best Song Category. He is from Pondicherry, and is a graduate in Physics.
